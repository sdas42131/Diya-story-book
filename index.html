Being Safe

Safety means to keep oneself away from any kind of danger.We must always follow the safety rules wherever we go-at home, in the playground, at school or anywhere else.

At Home

  • Do not open doors of your house to the starngers.
  • Always take permission of your elders before going out of the house.
  • Do not leave your toys,school bag,etc on the floor.You may fall over them and get hurt.
  • Do not play with sharp objects and electrical switches..
  • keep away from fire & do not play with matchsticks & matchboxes..
  • Never run on the staircase.

On the road.

  • Always walk on the pevement. cross the road only at the Zebra crossing.
  • Wait for the walk signal to turn green before crossing the road.
  • Cross the road with an elder.
  • Do not play or run on the road.

    • Travelling in a veichle

    • never put your hand or head out of a moving veichle. other veichles passing by may hit you.
    • Wear a sit belt while travelling in a car.
    • Always get in & get off the bus only at the bus stop. get off the bus only after it stops.
    • Do not disturb the driver.
